Welcome
Dear Colleagues and Friends,
The 3th International High-frequency Gravitational Waves Workshop  will be held at Southwest Jiaotong University, Chengdu, China, from 7th-9th, April. 2017. It is the third consecutive meeting in series which the first meetings was held in Virginia, USA in 2003 and the second one was held in Texas, USA, in 2007. Each of the past meetings have attracted many senior experts, young researchers, as well as graduate students, from Singapore and China and other countries.
  We cordially invite all the related theoretical and experimental physicists to participate this workshop and enjoy a valuable academic tour on high frequency gravational waves in Chengdu, China. The purpose of the meeting is to present and discuss their latest research advances in the following fields (but not limited):
  a. High frequency Gravitational Sources,
  b. Gravitational Wave Detections,
  c. Microwave Photonics，
  d. Gravitational wave astronomy and related Cosmology Probelms.

  All the submitted papers to the workshop will be selectively recommed to 《Gravitation and Cosmology》for publications. We are looking forward to hosting you at Chengdu, a beautiful and modernization city in the west of China!

Near to Jiuzhaigou Valley Scenic and Historic Interest Area
