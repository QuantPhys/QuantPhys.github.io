Dear Colleagues and Friends,
The 11th China-Singapore Joint Symposium on Research Frontiers will be held in Chengdu China during September 2-4, 2015, which is organized by Southwest Jiaotong University. We cordially invite theoretical and experimental physicists to participate in this workshop and enjoy the academic tour in China.
It is the eleventh consecutive meeting in series which the first  meetings was held in 2006. The past 6 meetings have attracted many experts, young researchers as well as graduate students from both the greater China region and overseas. The purpose of the meeting is to present and discuss their latest research advances in various fields of physics. The workshop is attracted by 15 experts and scholars from all over the world, such as National University of Singapore, National Chiao Tung University in Hsinchu, Taiwan, St. Louis, Washington University, the American National Standards Institute and so on.
We are looking forward to hosting you in Chengdu, China!
