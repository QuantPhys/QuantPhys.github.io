Dear Colleagues and Friends,
     The 11th China-Singapore Joint Symposium on Physical Frontiers will be held at  Southwest Jiaotong University, Chengdu, China, from 19th-21st, Sep. 2015. It is the eleventh consecutive meeting in series held firstly in 2006. Each of the past meetings have attracted many senior experts, young researchers, as well as graduate students, from Singapore and China and other countries.

    We cordially invite theoretical and experimental physicists, not limited from China and Singapore, to participate this workshop and enjoy the academic tour in Chengdu, China. The purpose of the meeting is to present and discuss their latest research advances in the following fields of physics (but not limited):

  Fundamental Quantum Physics and Quantum Information Science,

    Cold Atomic/Molecular Physics and Superconducting Electronics,

    Micro/Nano Scale Physics and Computational Physics, etc..

     We are looking forward to hosting you at Chengdu, a beautiful and  modernization city in the west of China!
